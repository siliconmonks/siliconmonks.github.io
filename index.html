Welcome to Silicon Monks

This space is built for curious minds drawn to the world of VLSI and semiconductor design. Whether you are just starting out or already working with chip architecture, verification, or physical design, you will find ideas here that aim to deepen your understanding without the noise.

Silicon Monks focuses on the fundamentals and the evolving landscape of integrated circuits. From transistor-level concepts to system-on-chip design, from RTL to layout, the goal is to explore how modern hardware is built and why it works the way it does.

This is not about rushing through trends. It is about slowing down, thinking clearly, and building strong intuition in a field where precision matters.

Take your time. Explore. Learn with intent.
